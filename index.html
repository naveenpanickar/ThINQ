Why Chennai First?

Chennai is India's strongest convergence of print-demand density and print-supply fragmentation. A Metro with 12,000+ registered print & signage units, 3 major IT corridors generating enterprise B2B demand, 6 universities creating institutional print volume, and a thriving SME culture. ThINQ's Q'nnect model maps directly onto this existing but unorganised ecosystem.

Competitive Moat

Chennai's print ecosystem is hyper-fragmented — no dominant aggregator exists. First-mover with verified partner network creates a defensible local data moat before national players notice.

Institutional Anchor Strategy

Target 3 large anchor clients (IT company, hospital chain, university) in Month 1–3. Each generates ₹15–40L annual procurement — funds partner growth and validates the platform publicly.

Partner Retention Flywheel

ThINQ's GST-ready invoicing, SLA dashboard, and recurring order flow create switching costs for partners. A verified ThINQ badge becomes a trust signal for their own B2B clients.

B2B · Core

Transaction Commission

2–5% commission on every PO value processed through ThINQ. Tiered by volume — enterprise clients at lower rates drive high absolute INR value.

Est. avg. order: ₹45,000–₹2L+
B2B · SaaS

Enterprise Subscription

Monthly SaaS fee for RFQ automation, multi-branch procurement dashboard, GST compliance suite, and spend analytics. Scalable per seat or per entity.

Pricing: ₹4,999–₹24,999/mo
B2C · Core

Marketplace Take-rate

8–15% platform fee on every consumer order fulfilled through ThINQ. Consumers pay for convenience, speed, and trust; partners pay nothing extra.

Est. consumer basket: ₹800–₹8,000
