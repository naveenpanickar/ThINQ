India's print ecosystem remains highly fragmented despite serving every major industry — manufacturing, retail, education, healthcare, IT services, events and packaging. While ordering experiences have digitised across most categories, print procurement continues to rely heavily on phone calls, WhatsApp messages, spreadsheets and local relationships.

ThINQ addresses this gap through a q'nnected marketplace model.
